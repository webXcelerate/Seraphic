Our Services

Registered Massage Therapy

Massage Therapy is the assessment and treatment of the soft tissue and joints of the body by hands-on manipulation. It can be used to treat a variety of conditions or as part of a maintenance program. Most registered massage therapists base their treatments on Traditional Swedish Massage which is a smooth, flowing style with the use of oil or cream that can soothe all your worries away. Tight muscles will relax, swelling will decrease, injuries will mend, stress and headaches will disappear.

Hot Stone Massage

You can achieve a dreamlike state while warm smooth lava stones are used to massage and are placed strategically along the body. The purifying and relaxing effects of this technique will leave your muscles re-energized and free of tension.

Reflexology

Foot massage with a whole body effect. Through application of pressure to specific points on your feet, circulation will improve, tension will ease and conditions of the body will normalize.

Reiki

A series of hand sweeps and a light touch releases blocked energy caused by illness, stress or injury and promotes a positive energy healing response.

Inducement and Pregnancy Massage

Massage can alleviate symptoms in every stage of pregnancy, including inducing labour when overdue.

Aromatherapy

Combining the power of Essential Oils with a Massage treatment can enhance the benefits of each while alleviating a variety of conditions.

Yoga

Practiced for the development of strength and flexibility. Both Hatha and Vinyasa types of Yoga are gentle ways to emphasize stability, regulate your breathing and explore the mobility of your spine.

What to Expect

For your first visit, please arrive 15 minutes early, as you will have to fill out confidential personal history and consent forms and be assessed by your therapist before your treatment begins.

The massage therapist will use massage lotion or oil during the treatment. You may undress to your comfort level. During the treatment, your privacy is maintained by the cover of a sheet and blanket. The only area undraped will be the one being worked on. The therapist will instruct you as to how to lie on the table and when to turn over.
