Our Philosophy

Seraphic Massage Therapy believes in treating you, the client, to health. Founded by 2 sisters and their mother that share the same name “Serafim” the clinic has always been based on family and that is how we treat all of our clients, as if they’ve come home to people who care about them. Our Massage Therapists are all registered with the College of Massage Therapists of Ontario specializing in a variety of treatment methods and treating in a friendly and relaxed environment.

Seraphic is dedicated to making your visit as stress free as possible by offering direct billing with a variety of Insurance companies. We deal directly with Green Shield Canada, Blue Cross and Great West Life. Our receptionists and a few of our therapists speak Portuguese fluently to accommodate the local clientele.
